2k25 Scripture Union Camp: Done and Dusted

Published on August 3, 2025

It’s over.
The 2025 SU Camp has ended.
And honestly, I still feel the weight of it—in a good way.

Thousands of people.
So many moments.
But somehow, I still found me in the middle of all the noise.

This wasn’t just a camp.
It was a reset.
It pulled me out of “build mode” for a bit and brought me back to soul mode.
No code. No launch plans. Just presence.

I didn’t try to meet everybody.
I didn’t try to shine.
I just showed up. Fully. Quietly.
And in that space, I got to breathe.

I met a few people. Not many. But the ones I did meet? Real.
I listened more than I talked.
I watched. Reflected. Prayed.
Wrote a few things down that I’m not ready to share yet—but I will, in time.

It’s wild that just being away from Rocjet for five days can make me see it clearer.
I’m still building. Still focused.
But now, with more peace.
More clarity.
More God.

If you were at camp too—thank you.
Whether we talked or not, you were part of the energy that made this one unforgettable.

If you weren’t—this is me just letting you in.
This was a moment for my blueprint. A page I’ll never erase.

Done and dusted… but definitely not forgotten.

—Calchiwo
