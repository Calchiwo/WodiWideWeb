BuiltByWodi® Projects Family

📅 Published on: August 14, 2025

✍️ Written by: Caleb Chikanka Wodi

For the past 3-4 months, I've been working on a series of projects which I refer to as BuiltByWodi® Projects because I believe:

"Projects teach more than any courses — they make knowledge stick."

I started programming at 15 years old on May 11, 2025, and since then — exactly 95 days later — I can testify that I've built real projects that have taught me essential skills.

If you're curious about the BuiltByWodi® Projects, here’s everything you should know:

As of August 14, 2025, there are only 5 BuiltByWodi® Projects, which I’ll explain in the order I began them.

Note: These projects are not associated with Rocjet Technologies Ltd — they are my personal collection of real-world projects.

WodiWebify (Launched on May 30, 2025)

Description: An encrypted note-taking platform.

Quick History: I built WodiWebify v1 using Flask and SQLite. The MVP didn’t work out, so I built a smaller “v1 mini” which succeeded. Later, I began v1.1.0 but decided to shift toward WodiWebify v2.

WodiC (Launched on June 27, 2025)

Description: A voice calculator with AI and scientific tools.

Quick History: Started as a simple voice calculator (MVP), then attempted v2 but will now launch WodiC v3 with a public GitHub release.

WodiWaitlist (Launched on July 28, 2025)

Description: A platform to collect early interest and signups for upcoming projects.

Quick History: Created to securely collect early email signups for future launches.

WodiWideWeb (Launched on July 31, 2025)

Description: My personal platform for documenting my journey, lessons, and projects.

Quick History: Launched with a rough UI, then gradually improved with added features.

WodiMemories (Launched on August 8, 2025)

Description: A personal photo gallery capturing life’s moments and milestones.

Quick History: Built to document memories alongside the journey — fully integrated into WodiWideWeb.
