My First Office

📍Happened on: Thursday, May 1, 2025

📅 Published on: August 08, 2025

🕒 Age: 15

✍️ Written by: Caleb Chikanka Wodi

It wasn’t fancy. It wasn’t polished. It wasn’t even complete.

But on Thursday, May 1st, 2025 — just one day after I started Rocjet Technologies Ltd — I made a bold move. I set up my first office in an uncompleted building near my home.

No chairs. No fan. No desk. Just concrete, silence, and vision.

That uncompleted building became my launchpad.

Each day, I showed up with the same tools: my phone, my notebook, and relentless consistency.

It was there I began learning how to code.
It was there I wrote my first lines.
It was there I trained my focus.

No comfort. No excuses. Just quiet dedication and a dream louder than the noise I wasn’t hearing.

I didn’t wait for perfection. I didn’t wait to “arrive.”

I started where I was.
I used what I had.
And that made all the difference.

Looking back, that unfinished building represents more than just a physical space — it symbolizes a mindset. A stepping stone. A declaration.

It’s where I decided that greatness doesn’t wait.
It’s where I built the roots of Rocjet.
It’s where I began becoming who I am.

“I founded my first company in an uncompleted building near my home.”

— Caleb C. Wodi (CALCHIWO)
