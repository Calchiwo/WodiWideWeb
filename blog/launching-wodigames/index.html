Launching WodiGames — My Passion for Game Development

📅 Published on: August 20, 2025

✍️ Written by: Caleb Chikanka Wodi

Today marks another milestone in my journey. I officially launched WodiGames, a new addition to my BuiltByWodi® Projects.

Gaming has always been more than just fun for me — it’s a space of creativity, problem-solving, and storytelling. With WodiGames, I’m bringing that passion to life by actually building real games — games that are lightweight, creative, and fun, yet designed with purpose.

Unlike my personal projects like WodiMemories or WodiWideWeb, WodiGames isn’t just for me. It’s a public gaming space, available across web and app, mobile and desktop, offline-first so that anyone can play. This is my way of blending digital play with interactive storytelling, making games that people everywhere can enjoy.

WodiGames now officially joins the BuiltByWodi® Projects family alongside:

  • WodiWideWeb — my personal storytelling platform.
  • WodiWaitlist — a waitlist platform for early sign-ups.
  • WodiMemories — a personal photo gallery.
  • WodiWebify — an encrypted note-taking platform.
  • WodiC — a voice calculator with AI and scientific functions.

And now,

WodiGames — a cross-platform gaming space built by me, open to everyone.

This is just the beginning. I’ll be creating real games, not just concepts, and sharing the journey as I grow in game development.
