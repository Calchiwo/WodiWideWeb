As the founder of Rocjet Technologies Ltd, I've always been driven by a passion for innovation and problem-solving. Our first product, RocjetReminder, was born out of a desire to help people stay on top of their goals and reminders. In this post, I'll take you through the journey of RocjetReminder, from its early days to the upcoming v3 launch.

The Early Days

I started building the earliest version of RocjetReminder's MVP in my uncompleted building office, where I founded Rocjet Technologies Ltd. On June 21, 2025, I launched the web application version, a simple yet effective Progressive Web Application (PWA) with sign-up, sign-in, and goal-setting features. The response was encouraging, and I knew I was onto something.

Iterating and Improving

Just four days later, on June 25, 2025, I launched RocjetReminder MVP V2, built using AIDE and Firebase on my Android phone. This version combined reminder notifications with goal-setting features, taking the app to the next level.

The Next Chapter

Now, I'm working on RocjetReminder v3, which will be launched before September. This time, I'm taking a different approach. Instead of shipping it into the void of the internet, I'm building it as a completely offline app that includes:

  • Alarm setting features
  • Goal setting features
  • Reminder notifications

What's New in v3?

The biggest change in v3 is the app's ability to function offline, making it more reliable and accessible to users. I'm excited to bring this version to real people's hands, and I'm confident that it will make a significant impact.

Stay Tuned

I'm eager to share RocjetReminder v3 with the world, and I invite you to join me on this journey. Follow my storytelling platform for updates, behind-the-scenes insights, and sneak peeks into the world of Rocjet Technologies Ltd.

Thanks for reading, and I look forward to sharing RocjetReminder v3 with you soon!
