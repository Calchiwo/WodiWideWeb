📸 Introducing WodiMemories — A Visual Journey Begins

📅 Launch Date: August 08, 2025

✍️ Written by: Caleb Chikanka Wodi

I’m proud to officially introduce WodiMemories, the newest member of the BuiltByWodi® Projects Family.

WodiMemories is more than a photo gallery — it’s a story-driven collection of moments that trace my journey from humble beginnings to meaningful milestones. Every image has its own story, and together, they create a timeline of experiences that define me.

Built with simplicity, elegance, and heart, WodiMemories was designed to feel personal yet timeless. Whether you’re viewing it on your phone, tablet, or desktop, it’s clean, responsive, and immersive.

🌟 Key Highlights

  • Story-Driven Captions — Every photo comes with context, dates, or short notes.
  • Timeline Navigation — Travel through my life in chronological order.
  • Lightbox View — Click an image for a full-size, detailed view.
  • Part of BuiltByWodi® Projects Family — Branded to match WodiWideWeb, WodiC, WodiWebify, and WodiWaitlist.

🌐 Integration with WodiWideWeb

WodiMemories stands as its own independent website and GitHub repository for flexibility and creative freedom, but it’s also fully integrated into WodiWideWeb. Click the WodiMemories button on WodiWideWeb, and you’ll be redirected straight into the gallery.
