The Day After Launch — Building My First WodiGame

📅 Published on: August 21, 2025

✍️ Written by: Caleb Chikanka Wodi

Yesterday, August 20, I officially launched WodiGames. It marked the beginning of a new chapter in my journey — not just announcing the idea, but actually bringing games to life.

Today, I’m excited to share the very first game I’ve built under WodiGames: a simple yet powerful Go Game.

You can play it here:
👉 wodigames.netlify.app

This game was created entirely with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no shortcuts — just the raw web technologies that power the internet. For me, it’s more than a game; it’s proof that from the very basics, something fun and interactive can come alive.

And this is only the beginning. WodiGames is meant to grow into a collection of playful, lightweight, and creative experiences. More games are coming — each one built with passion, curiosity, and the drive to make gaming accessible to everyone.

This journey is just getting started.

— Caleb Chikanka Wodi
