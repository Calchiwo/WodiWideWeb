How I Started Rocjet Technologies Ltd

📍Happened on: Wednesday, April 30, 2025

Published on: August 08, 2025

🕒Age: 15

✍️Written by: Caleb Chikanka Wodi

It wasn’t just another evening. It was the evening.

On Wednesday, April 30th, 2025 — the same day I came back from writing my JAMB exam — something inside me shifted. I didn’t want to wait anymore. I had carried a dream for too long. That night, I officially started Rocjet Technologies Ltd.

It was just me, my God, my phone, my logbook, my boldness — and a vision too big to ignore.

The birth of Rocjet wasn’t random

I had already designed the logo. I already had the name.

The name “Rocjet” was an intentional misspelling of “Rocket” — to stand out, to feel original, to represent me. But because I was deep into JAMB preparations, I hadn’t acted on it.

Until that evening.

That night, I made my move. I created the business socials, picked the tools I needed, and opened my logbook like it was a launchpad.

No confusion. No hesitation. Just momentum.

It wasn’t about perfection — it was about starting

There was no grand office. No funding. No team. Just a 15-year-old with fire in his bones and a global vision in his heart.

I didn’t wait to “grow up.” I didn’t wait for permission. I built. I started.

And that’s how Rocjet Technologies Ltd was born.

“Start before you’re ready. Dream before they believe you. Build before they see it.”
— Caleb C. Wodi (CALCHIWO)
