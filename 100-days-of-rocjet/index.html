100 Days of Building Rocjet Technologies Ltd

📅 Published on: August 7, 2025

🕒 Age: 15

✍️ Written by: Caleb Chikanka Wodi

Today marks exactly the 100th day since I began this journey of building Rocjet Technologies Ltd, and I’m so excited about how far I’ve come

As a 15-year-old solo founder passionate about innovation, entrepreneurship, and storytelling, I’ve learned that success in this journey is rooted in three key principles:

  • 👉 Speed
  • 👉 Clarity
  • 👉 Resilience

I’m incredibly proud of what I’ve achieved so far, and I’m even more excited for what lies ahead.

🚧 The Beginning

I founded Rocjet Technologies Ltd on April 30, 2025, in an uncompleted building near my home. With nothing but a vision and determination to turn my ideas into real, practical tools.

Since then, I’ve built and launched several early projects, including:

  • 🔐 WodiWebify — An encrypted note-taking web application
  • 🧮 WodiC — A voice-powered calculator with AI and scientific tools
  • 📬 WodiWaitlist — A waitlist platform for collecting email sign-ups ahead of my future launches
  • 🌍 WodiWideWeb — A personal storytelling platform where I document my journey, reflections, lessons, and projects

📚 Lessons Learned

These past 100 days haven’t been easy. I’ve faced challenges like:

  • Debugging bugs that seemed impossible
  • Managing my time between learning, building, and documenting
  • Staying focused when distractions come

But I’ve learned to persevere. I’ve realized that breaking down complex problems into smaller tasks is the secret to making consistent progress.

🚀 What’s Next?

For the next 100 days, I plan to:

  • 🚀 Launch WodiC v3 with more advanced AI tools
  • 📈 Grow WodiWaitlist and gather user feedback
  • ✍️ Keep documenting my journey through WodiWideWeb

💡 Personal Growth

Building Rocjet Technologies Ltd has taught me:

  • How to solve problems with clarity
  • How to think critically and act intentionally
  • How to believe in myself, even when things get tough

I’ve grown more confident in my abilities — as a developer, as a storyteller, and as a founder.
